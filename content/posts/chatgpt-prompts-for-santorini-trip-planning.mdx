## The foundation prompt for Santorini planning

Start with context, not generic questions. ChatGPT performs better when you establish your travel style, constraints, and priorities upfront.

Try this foundational prompt:

"I'm planning a 6-day trip to Santorini in late September. I'm a 30-something couple who values authentic experiences over Instagram spots, though we want at least one sunset dinner at Ambrosia Restaurant in Oia. Budget is $4,000 total including flights from NYC. We prefer boutique accommodations like Perivolas or Mystique Resort and hate crowds. Create a detailed itinerary with specific recommendations for where to stay, eat, and explore."

This gives ChatGPT your demographics, timing, budget, and preferences—the building blocks for relevant suggestions. Generic prompts like "plan my Santorini trip" produce generic listicle content you could find anywhere.

## Drilling down with specific scenario prompts

Once you have a foundation, get tactical. ChatGPT excels at solving specific travel puzzles when you frame them clearly.

**For timing and seasonality:**
"Compare visiting Santorini in mid-May versus late September. Factor in weather (May averages 23°C, September 25°C), crowds at Oia Castle, prices for hotels in Imerovigli, and restaurant availability in Pyrgos village. Which shoulder season offers better value for someone who wants warm weather but fewer tourists at Red Beach?"

**For neighborhood selection:**
"I want to stay in Oia for one night at Canaves Oia Epitome for the sunset, but where should I base myself for the other 4 nights? I want authentic Greek culture, good restaurants within walking distance of places like Selene or Metaxi Mas, and easy access to Kamari Beach. Compare Fira's proximity to the cable car, Imerovigli's caldera position, and Pyrgos's traditional architecture with specific pros and cons."

**For logistics planning:**
"Create a day-by-day transportation plan for Santorini without a rental car. Include specific routes (Fira to Oia bus takes 20 minutes), timing for the KTEL bus system, taxi costs from airport to Imerovigli (approximately €25), and walking distances between Santo Wines winery, Red Beach, and Akrotiri archaeological site."

> "The best ChatGPT prompts solve specific problems rather than asking for generic travel advice you could Google in five minutes."

## Advanced prompts for authentic experiences

Generic Santorini guides focus on the same dozen sunset spots and cave hotels. Use ChatGPT to dig deeper into authentic experiences that match your interests.

**For food and wine:**
"I want to eat where locals eat in Santorini, not tourist restaurants in Fira's main square. Recommend 3 family-run tavernas like Roka in Imerovigli or Lucky's Souvlakis in Fira, plus 2 wineries such as Domaine Sigalas or Venetsanos that focus on Assyrtiko grape education over Instagram moments. Include specific dishes like fava me koukia and why these places matter to Greek food culture."

**For cultural depth:**
"Create a Santorini itinerary focused on the island's volcanic history at the Museum of Prehistoric Thera and traditional architecture in Megalochori village. Include the Maritime Museum in Oia, Akrotiri excavation site's Minoan ruins, and villages like Emporio that showcase authentic Cycladic life beyond the typical Santo Wines sunset cruise recommendations."

**For lesser-known spots:**
"What are 5 specific spots in Santorini that most tourists miss? Include precise locations like the lighthouse at Akrotiri's southern tip, Prophet Elias Monastery's panoramic viewpoint, or the black pebbles at Vlychada Beach, why they're special, and the best time of day to visit. I want authentic experiences, not crowded viewpoints that are actually packed by 4pm."

These prompts push past surface-level recommendations toward genuine local insights.
